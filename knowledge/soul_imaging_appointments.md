3. Email **info@soulimaging.com.au**

## Preparation Details
Depending on the scan type, specific preparations might be needed. High-level rules generally include:
- **Ultrasound (Abdomen/Pelvis):** May require fasting or a full bladder. Please ask our staff when booking.
- **CT Scan (with Contrast):** May require fasting and prior blood tests (kidney function). 
- **X-Ray:** Generally no special prep required just ensure no metal is on the direct imaging zone.

## Cancellation / No-Shows
If you cannot make your scheduled appointment, we kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice so we can offer the slot to someone else in need.

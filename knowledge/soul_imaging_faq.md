# Soul Imaging — Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

## 1. Do I need a referral to get a scan?
Yes, you generally require a referral from a GP, specialist, or chiropractor to undergo any diagnostic imaging. We **accept all referrals** regardless of the company or clinic style on the paper header.

## 2. Do you offer Bulk Billing?
We offer bulk billing for most Medicare-eligible scans. Please present your Medicare card upon arrival.

## 3. Do I need an appointment?
- **X-Ray:** No appointment needed. Walk-ins are welcome.
- **Non-Contrast CT Scan:** No appointment needed. Walk-ins are welcome.
- **Ultrasound:** Walk-ins are possible depending on preparation requirements, but we highly recommend scheduling ahead of time to secure an immediate slot.

## 4. How long does it take to get reports?
Patient reports are sent securely to the referring doctor within 24 to 48 hours. If your case is urgent, please let us know when you arrive so we can expedite it.

## 5. Can I access my own images?
Yes! You can view your images and reports via the **"Soul Imaging Patient Portal"** app on your smartphone or tablet (powered by Zed Technologies). Instructions to download and sign up are provided at the clinic.

## 6. Do you have On-Call Services?
We offer extended on-call X-Ray & CT services for emergencies and urgent diagnostics outside normal hours:
- Mon–Fri: 5 PM – 10 PM
- Sat: 3 PM – 10 PM

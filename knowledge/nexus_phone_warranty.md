# Nexus-X Smartphone — Official Warranty Policy (2026)

## ⏳ Warranty Duration
The Nexus-X comes with a unique **28-month limited hardware warranty** from the date of first boot. This is 10 months longer than our competitors.

## 💧 Liquid Damage Policy
While the Nexus-X is IP68 rated, water damage is only covered if the immersion was less than **2 meters for under 15 minutes**. Any exposure to saltwater immediately voids the warranty.

## 🎁 The "Nexus Care" First 60 Days
For the first **60 days**, we offer a "Zero-Cost Screen Replacement" for any accidental cracks. No questions asked. This applies only once per device.

## 🌍 International Claims
If you are traveling in **Japan, Singapore, or Germany**, you can walk into any authorized carrier and get an on-the-spot repair. For all other countries, the device must be mailed back to our hub in Dallas, Texas.

## 🛠️ Modding & Rooting
The warranty is NOT voided by rooting or installing custom ROMs, as long as the hardware fault is not a direct result of software overvolting. We support developer freedom!

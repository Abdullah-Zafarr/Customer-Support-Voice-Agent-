# Soul Imaging — Diagnostic Services

We offer a range of high-definition diagnostic imaging services utilizing advanced technology for precise diagnostics.

## 1. General X-Ray
- High-definition imaging for bone, chest, and torso diagnostics.
- Available as a walk-in service during normal hours and on-call.
- Quick, safe, and efficient.

## 2. CT Scanning (Computed Tomography)
- Advanced scanners provide detailed cross-sectional images of body structures.
- Suitable for head, chest, abdomen, spine, and extremity imaging.
- Non-contrast CT scans are available on a walk-in basis.

## 3. Ultrasound
We provide comprehensive ultrasound exams leveraging advanced techniques for better visualization:
- **General & Specialty Ultrasounds:** Tailored to specific medical needs.
- **Shear-Wave Elastography:** For stiffness assessment (e.g., liver fibrosis).
- **Quantitative Fat Analysis:** Precise measurement of organ fat content.
- **B Flow & Microvascular Flow:** Advanced visualization for blood flow without needing contrast agents, ensuring higher safety and precision.
- *Note:* While some ultrasounds can be done as walk-ins, we highly recommend calling ahead to schedule the exact time to avoid waiting.
